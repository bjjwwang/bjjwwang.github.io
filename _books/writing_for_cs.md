The essential guide for CS researchers on how to write clearly and effectively. Zobel covers everything from structuring a paper, presenting algorithms and mathematics, to handling the review process. A must-read for any PhD student — it changed how I think about communicating research. Practical, concise, and full of examples of what to do (and what not to do).
