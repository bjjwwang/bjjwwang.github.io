Harari traces how information networks — from ancient mythologies to modern AI — have shaped civilizations. The book asks a critical question: does more information lead to more truth, or can it just as easily fuel delusion? A thought-provoking read that connects the dots between bureaucracy, algorithms, and the future of human decision-making. Especially relevant for anyone working in tech who wants to think beyond code.
