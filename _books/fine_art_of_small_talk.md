Small talk is an underrated skill, especially in academia and industry networking. Debra Fine breaks down conversational techniques into actionable steps — how to start, sustain, and gracefully exit conversations. Useful for conferences, interviews, and everyday interactions. A quick read that pays dividends in social confidence.
